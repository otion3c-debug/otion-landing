Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026

Otion LLC ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI business solutions including AI agent deployment, website development, and the ScheduleLink scheduling platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account & business information — name, email address, phone number, business name, and billing details.
  • Customer information — when clients book through our services, we receive their name, phone number, email, and appointment or service details.
  • SMS & messaging data — message content, delivery status, and metadata (timestamps, sender/recipient phone numbers) for messages sent or received through our services.
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Website usage data — standard analytics including pages visited and referring URLs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and improving our AI agent, website, and scheduling services
  • Sending SMS notifications, appointment confirmations, and reminders
  • Enabling two-way messaging between service providers and their customers
  • Processing payments via Stripe
  • Calendar synchronization (Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook)
  • Customer support and service delivery
  • Legal compliance and fraud prevention

3. SMS & Text Messaging

Our services use SMS to send appointment confirmations, reminders, and facilitate communication between businesses and their clients.

  • Message frequency — varies based on usage. Typical volume is 2–6 messages per booking. You may opt out at any time.
  • Opt-out — Reply STOP, CANCEL, END, or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to stop receiving messages. You will receive one final confirmation message.
  • Help — Reply HELP or INFO for assistance.
  • Cost — Message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for pricing.
  • No third-party sharing of consent — We do not share, sell, or transfer mobile information, opt-in data, or messaging consent to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is used exclusively to provide the messaging service you requested.

SMS is transmitted through Twilio Inc. Twilio processes message content in accordance with their own privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Twilio — SMS delivery and phone number management
  • Stripe — Payment processing and Connect onboarding
  • Google — Workspace email, Calendar/APIs
  • Microsoft — Outlook Calendar (Graph API)
  • Vercel — Web hosting
  • Neon (PostgreSQL) — Database hosting

5. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share mobile information or messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information is shared only as necessary to operate our services (e.g., Twilio for SMS, Stripe for payments) or when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account is active or as needed to provide services. SMS content and metadata are retained for up to 12 months. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time
  • Revoke OAuth calendar access
  • Request a copy of your data

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or through our website.
